Quick Guide

Care tips for decorative plants

Simple habits that keep your plants healthy, clean, and looking stunning in any space.

Light

Bright indirect light works best for most indoor decorative plants.

Water

Water only when the top soil feels dry. Avoid overwatering.

Pot & Drainage

Choose pots with drainage holes to keep roots healthy and fresh.

Leaf Care

Wipe leaves gently to remove dust and keep them vibrant.

Rotate Weekly

Turn your plant weekly so it grows evenly and keeps a balanced shape.

Clean Design

Match pot colors to your room for a calm, premium decorative look.
